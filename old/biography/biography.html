NINA VITANOVA VLAHOVA

     

Multimedia Images

Dance-Performance

Concept Art

Born january 6, 1966 in Sofia, Bulgaria

Education to baccalauriat in Zurich, Switzerland Classical ballet from age 6 to 12

Studies in Paris, France:

- theatre and mouvement school, Jacques Lecoq (2 years)
- school of contemporary dance with Peter Goss (4 years)
(still taking classes with Peter Goss and masterclasses in Caroline Carlson's studio 3 months a year.)
- studies of fine art in the LEM (laboratoire experimental de l’education du mouvement) where abstraction in painting and sculpture as well as in scenic objects was taught (3years)


EXHIBITIONS, VIDEO WORKS AND PERFORMANCES:

   
1987 - awarded the“stipendia of fine art of the canton of Zurich“
1989 - awarded the “stipendia of fine art of the city of Zurich“
- collective exhibition in the Kunsthaus Zurich (the national museum)
1990 - collective exhibition in the Helmhaus Zurich (the main contemporary art museum of zurich)
- the trilogy « ivan karamasov »goes over to the privat collection of Malkolm Forbes
1991 - personal exhibition and dance-performance with scenique objects in the Steglitzer Bahnhof Gallery, Berlin
- personal exhibition in the Thea Fischer Rheinhard Gallery, Berlin.
1992 - personal exhibition and dance-performance in the pavillon , ’optika’ of the ’vdnh ’, Moscow
1993 - joint creation with the director Konstantin Mitniev (parrallel kino) of the video-art film "screendream" (on betacam) combining acting, dancing and video-effects
1994/95 - multiple video-installations and multimedia-performances in collaboration with Kirill Preobrazenskji (concept-artist + video-artist) and Lev Rubinstein (conceptual poet)
- shows in the Contemporary Art Center, Moscow and on the Festival of Poetry in St Petersburg.
- performance with Barry Schwarz, at the Fine-Art University, Hamburg
1996 - dance-performance in the multimedia-performance ‘roma-ma-chine’ on the art-project Das Treuanische Schiff in the port of Hamburg in collaboration with Mike Hentz (multimedia-artist)
1997 - video-film (betacam) Surgery in Moscow in collaboration with Christophe Peter (director)
1998/99 - preparation for the concept-art project of exhibitions and performances by 15 suisse and russian artists : Mental Landscapes
1999 - manager (+video and dance) of the project mental landscapes in :
Shed im eisenwerk, Frauenfeld (contemporary-art museum)
Can, Neuchatel (contemporary-art museum)
Helmhaus, Zurich (national museum of contemporary-art)
- dance-performance in the framework of the exhibition Nouveau Novosibirsk by Andrei Molodkin and Alexei Beliayev Guintovt in the Chapelle Saint Louis de la Pitie Salpetriere, Paris
2000/02 - writing the concept and texts for an interactive text-structure with multi-medial extensions : "tigremask" (in german)
2001 - dance-performance on the theme of the collors: red, blue, white, yellow in the museum of concrete and constructive art, Zurich
2002/04 - combined work in the different disciplines of text/video/dance/painting while creating the multimedial images for the theme brain extension and preparation of a video/dance performance on the same theme with video-artist Olga Kymeger and dancers Andreji Andrjanov + Genja Andrianova.
